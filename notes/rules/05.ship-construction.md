# 22. Ship Construction

**22.1** Players build and maintain ships in **census order**. Building a ship costs **two tokens**, paid either:
- From treasury,
- By a levy of tokens from the area where the ship is being constructed, or
- A combination of the two.

> **Military:** Players who hold Military always build and maintain ships **after** players who do not hold Military. Order between Military holders is determined normally by census order.

**22.2 Placement:**
- A ship financed **completely from treasury** may be placed in any area containing at least one of the player's units.
- A ship built **wholly or partially by levy** must be placed in the area being levied.
- All tokens spent on ships are returned to stock.

**22.3 Maintenance:** Ships already in play must be maintained at a cost of **one token per turn** from treasury or by a levy of one token from the area the ship occupies. Ships which are not maintained are **immediately returned to stock**. A player may remove a ship from the board by not paying maintenance, then build it in a different area in the same phase.

**22.4** A player may **not have more than four ships** in play at one time.
