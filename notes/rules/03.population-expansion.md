# 20. Population Expansion

**20.1** Each player adds:
- **One token** to every area containing one of his tokens
- **Two tokens** to every area already containing two or more of his tokens

Tokens are **never** added to areas with cities. Tokens **may** be added in excess of an area's population limit.

**20.2** If a player does not have sufficient tokens in stock to complete full population expansion, he divides what he has among eligible areas as he wishes. Otherwise, population expansion is **automatic and may not be voluntarily curtailed**.

**20.3** Population is increased in **A.S.T. order**. If increases do not affect other players, this activity may be carried out simultaneously.
