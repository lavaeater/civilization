# 25. City Construction

**25.1** City construction takes place after all conflict is resolved. A city may be built in any land area. Each land area may contain **only one city**. No player may have more than **nine cities** on the board at any one time.

**25.2 Construction requirements:**
- In an area **with a city site**: at least **six tokens** required
- In an area **without a city site**: at least **twelve tokens** required

Tokens used to construct cities are returned to stock.

> **25.3 Architecture:** Players who hold Architecture may use tokens from their **treasury** to assist in building **one city per turn**. At least half of the tokens used must be on-board tokens; the remainder may come from treasury. Architecture may **not** be used to construct cities in areas containing tokens belonging to another player or Barbarians.
