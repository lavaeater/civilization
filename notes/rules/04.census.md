# 21. Census

**21.1** Each player counts the number of **tokens** on the board. Cities and ships are **not** counted.

- Except for players who hold **Military**, the player with the **most tokens** builds ships first and moves first in the movement phase.
- Players follow in order of **decreasing token strength**.

**21.2** Each player's census marker is placed on the Census Track at the space corresponding to his population. This establishes the order of movement for the turn. Ties are resolved by the number on the census marker (A.S.T. order).
