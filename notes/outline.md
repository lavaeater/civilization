# Outline

## Intro

This document contains specs, ideas, links and todo-lists pertaining to this project, the game of Advanced Civilization.

## The Next Steps

What is left to do. Below are the identified areas:

- Civilization Cards and Effects
- Ships - construction, maintenance, boarding, travel over water and open water.
- AST Progress at end of rounds
- Improved AI - a scoring-based AI with weighted priorities to simulate strategies and personalities. Also consider memories and the prisoner's dilemma, i.e., for trading (the only area where this applies), make sure different players employ different strategies for deception when it comes to extra cards.
- Expand trade so they can contain more than three cards - putting emphasis on trust between players.
- Multiplayer support
- Network play
- Multi-platform play
